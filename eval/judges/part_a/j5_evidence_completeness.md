# J5 Evidence Completeness

Score only whether the appeal tells the filer what evidence is needed.

5: The appeal includes a case-specific evidence checklist with attachment
status or clear next actions. It names the records needed for this denial type,
such as clinical notes, prior treatment history, peer-to-peer records, reviewer
credentials, plan language, timestamps, or letters of medical necessity.

3: The appeal asks for medical records or provider support but is not precise
about which records matter.

1: No evidence checklist, or it claims evidence is attached when the case does
not support that.

Quote evidence first, then output score 1, 3, or 5 as JSON.
