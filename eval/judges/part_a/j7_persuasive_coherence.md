# J7 Persuasive Coherence & Tone

Score only coherence, structure, and tone.

5: The appeal has a clear flow: identifies the denial, states the request,
rebuts the denial, names the supporting evidence, and closes with a specific
requested action. Tone is calm, factual, and dignified. No exclamation marks.

3: The appeal has a recognizable argument but paragraphs feel patched together
or the request is not crisp.

1: The appeal is confusing, emotionally escalated, contradictory, or unclear
about what the insurer should do.

Length is not quality. Quote evidence first, then output score 1, 3, or 5 as
JSON.
