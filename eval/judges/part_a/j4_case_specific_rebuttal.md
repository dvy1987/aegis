# J4 Case-Specific Clinical Rebuttal

Score only whether the appeal uses this case's actual clinical facts.

5: Diagnosis, requested treatment, failed alternatives, duration, severity, and
clinical trajectory are woven into the argument. The argument cannot be reused
for another case without substantial rewriting.

3: The appeal mentions the diagnosis and treatment but mostly argues at the
denial-category level.

1: Generic form letter. Clinical facts are absent, wrong, or only listed in a
header/checklist.

Do not score citations or appeal-vector capture. Quote evidence first, then
output score 1, 3, or 5 as JSON.
