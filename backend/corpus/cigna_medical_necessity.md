# Cigna Medical Necessity Appeal Guidelines

When Cigna denies a claim for medical necessity, the appeal must explicitly demonstrate how the requested service meets the specific criteria defined by Cigna's coverage policies. 

## Appeal Requirements
1. **Clinical documentation:** Must include the patient's full medical history relevant to the requested service.
2. **Prior treatments:** Must document failure of conservative or alternative therapies (step-therapy requirements).
3. **Peer-reviewed literature:** Appeals citing off-label or investigational uses must be supported by at least two peer-reviewed clinical studies.
4. **MCG Criteria:** If the denial was based on Milliman Care Guidelines (MCG), the appeal must rebut the specific MCG criteria cited in the denial letter.
