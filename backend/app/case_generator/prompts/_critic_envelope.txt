CRITIC OUTPUT FORMAT — every critic in this swarm returns this exact JSON shape (no markdown, no backticks):
{
  "dimension": "<critic-id>",
  "reasoning": "<chain-of-thought, 50-200 words, MUST come before the score and quote specific text>",
  "score": <1 or 3 or 5> OR "PASS" OR "FAIL",
  "confidence": 0.0-1.0,
  "evidence_quotes": ["<exact quote(s) from the artifact>"],
  "improvement": "<one specific actionable fix, or null if score=5 or PASS>"
}

ALPHAEVAL RULES:
- Forced anchors only: 1, 3, or 5. No 2s or 4s.
- Binary hard gates return only "PASS" or "FAIL".
- Reason BEFORE you score (CoT first). Never write the score then the rationale.
- Score ONLY your single dimension. Do not bleed into other concerns.
- Quote the exact text that justifies your score.
